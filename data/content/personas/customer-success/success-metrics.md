# Customer Success Metrics and KPI Framework

## Customer Success Metrics Hierarchy

### Strategic Level Metrics (Executive Dashboard)

#### North Star Metrics
- **Net Revenue Retention (NRR)**: Primary indicator of customer success and growth
- **Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)**: Long-term financial value of customer relationships
- **Customer Acquisition Cost Payback**: Time to recover customer acquisition investment
- **Annual Recurring Revenue (ARR) Growth**: Year-over-year revenue expansion
- **Customer Health Score**: Composite indicator of account status and risk

#### Financial Performance Metrics
```
Net Revenue Retention = (Starting ARR + Expansion - Downgrades - Churn) / Starting ARR × 100%

Customer Lifetime Value = (Average Revenue per Customer × Gross Margin) / Churn Rate

Customer Acquisition Cost Payback = Customer Acquisition Cost / (Monthly Recurring Revenue × Gross Margin %)

Gross Revenue Retention = (Starting ARR - Churn) / Starting ARR × 100%
```

### Operational Level Metrics (Team Performance)

#### Customer Engagement Metrics
- **Product Adoption Rate**: Percentage of customers using core features
- **Feature Adoption Depth**: Average number of features used per customer
- **Daily/Monthly Active Users**: Regular engagement and usage patterns
- **Time Spent in Product**: Session duration and interaction depth
- **Workflow Completion Rate**: Success rate of core customer processes

#### Relationship Quality Metrics
- **Customer Satisfaction Score (CSAT)**: Satisfaction with customer success interactions
- **Net Promoter Score (NPS)**: Likelihood to recommend product/service
- **Customer Effort Score (CES)**: Ease of achieving desired outcomes
- **Executive Sponsor Engagement**: C-level involvement and commitment
- **Champion Network Strength**: Internal advocates and power users

### Leading Indicator Metrics (Predictive)

#### Health and Risk Indicators
- **Login Frequency Trend**: Changes in user engagement patterns
- **Support Ticket Volume**: Increase in help requests or issues
- **Training Participation**: Engagement with educational resources
- **User Expansion Rate**: Growth in number of users per account
- **Integration Adoption**: Usage of API connections and workflows

#### Early Warning Signals
- **Declining Usage Patterns**: Decrease in key activity metrics
- **Missing Stakeholder Engagement**: Loss of key contacts or champions
- **Delayed Milestone Achievement**: Behind schedule on implementation goals
- **Negative Feedback Trends**: Increasing complaints or dissatisfaction
- **Contract or Billing Issues**: Payment problems or contract disputes

## Customer Health Scoring Framework

### Composite Health Score Model

#### Health Score Components and Weighting
```
Overall Health Score = Product Usage (40%) + Business Outcomes (25%) + 
                      Relationship Quality (20%) + Account Growth (15%)

Scoring Methodology:
- Each component scored 0-100
- Weighted average calculation
- Monthly score updates
- Trend analysis over time
```

#### Product Usage Scoring (40% weight)
- **Login Frequency**: Regular engagement vs. sporadic usage
- **Feature Adoption**: Breadth of feature utilization
- **Session Duration**: Depth of engagement per session  
- **Workflow Completion**: Success rate of key processes
- **Data Quality**: Completeness and accuracy of user data

#### Business Outcomes Scoring (25% weight)
- **Goal Achievement**: Progress toward stated business objectives
- **ROI Realization**: Measurable business value and return on investment
- **Time to Value**: Speed of achieving meaningful outcomes
- **Success Milestone Completion**: Progress through implementation phases
- **Business Impact Metrics**: KPI improvements attributable to product

#### Relationship Quality Scoring (20% weight)
- **Satisfaction Scores**: CSAT, NPS, and feedback ratings
- **Communication Frequency**: Regular interaction with success team
- **Executive Engagement**: C-level involvement and sponsorship
- **Champion Strength**: Internal advocacy and expansion support
- **Escalation History**: Frequency and severity of issues or complaints

#### Account Growth Scoring (15% weight)
- **User Growth**: Expansion in number of active users
- **Feature Expansion**: Adoption of additional product capabilities
- **Integration Growth**: Increased API usage and workflow automation
- **Contract Expansion**: Upselling and cross-selling success
- **Referral Generation**: Customer advocacy and reference participation

### Health Score Thresholds and Actions

#### Health Score Categories
- **90-100 (Excellent - Green)**: High satisfaction, strong growth potential
- **70-89 (Good - Yellow)**: Stable but monitor for optimization opportunities
- **50-69 (At Risk - Orange)**: Intervention required, risk mitigation needed
- **0-49 (Critical - Red)**: Immediate escalation, retention threat

#### Automated Response Triggers
- **Score Drop >20 points**: Automatic CSM notification and review
- **Orange Zone Entry**: Structured intervention workflow activation
- **Red Zone Entry**: Executive escalation and retention team involvement
- **Consecutive Decline**: Multi-month negative trend analysis and action

## Success Metrics by Customer Lifecycle Stage

### Onboarding Stage Metrics (0-90 days)

#### Activation Metrics
- **Time to First Value**: Duration until initial meaningful outcome
- **Onboarding Completion Rate**: Percentage completing setup milestones
- **Feature Adoption Velocity**: Speed of core feature adoption
- **User Activation Rate**: Percentage of provisioned users actively engaged
- **Implementation Timeline Adherence**: Progress against planned schedule

#### Early Success Indicators
- **Quick Win Achievement**: Early positive outcomes and momentum
- **Training Completion**: Participation in onboarding education
- **Support Interaction Quality**: Resolution rate and satisfaction
- **Stakeholder Engagement**: Key contact participation and enthusiasm
- **Initial Health Score**: Baseline establishment for future tracking

### Growth Stage Metrics (3-12 months)

#### Expansion Metrics
- **Feature Adoption Growth**: Increase in breadth of product utilization
- **User Base Expansion**: Growth in active user count
- **Usage Depth Metrics**: Increase in advanced feature adoption
- **Integration Implementation**: API connections and workflow automation
- **Business Value Realization**: Measurable ROI and outcome achievement

#### Maturation Indicators
- **Self-Sufficiency Rate**: Reduced dependence on support resources
- **Advanced Feature Usage**: Adoption of sophisticated capabilities
- **Process Optimization**: Workflow efficiency improvements
- **Team Collaboration**: Multi-user and cross-team adoption
- **Success Story Development**: Case study and reference potential

### Renewal Stage Metrics (Pre-contract renewal)

#### Retention Predictors
- **Renewal Probability Score**: Likelihood of contract continuation
- **Expansion Opportunity**: Upselling and cross-selling potential
- **Executive Sponsorship**: C-level commitment and satisfaction
- **Business Dependency**: Mission-critical usage and integration depth
- **Competitive Evaluation**: Assessment of alternative solutions

#### Renewal Success Factors
- **ROI Demonstration**: Clear business value and financial impact
- **Future Value Alignment**: Roadmap alignment with customer needs
- **Relationship Strength**: Trust, satisfaction, and communication quality
- **Success Momentum**: Positive trend in key success metrics
- **Strategic Partnership**: Long-term mutual value alignment

## Reporting and Dashboard Framework

### Executive Reporting Dashboard

#### Key Performance Summary
- **Monthly/Quarterly Business Reviews**: Strategic performance assessment
- **NRR and Growth Trends**: Revenue retention and expansion metrics
- **Customer Health Distribution**: Portfolio health overview and trends
- **Churn Analysis**: Lost customer impact and root cause analysis
- **Success Program ROI**: Investment return and business impact

#### Strategic Insights
- **Segmentation Analysis**: Performance by customer segment and vertical
- **Cohort Performance**: Retention and expansion by acquisition period
- **Competitive Analysis**: Win/loss patterns and market positioning
- **Product Adoption Trends**: Feature usage patterns and opportunity identification
- **Market Opportunity**: Expansion potential and growth projections

### Operational Reporting Dashboard

#### CSM Performance Metrics
- **Portfolio Health**: Distribution of health scores across managed accounts
- **Account Growth**: Revenue expansion and user growth achievements
- **Activity Metrics**: Customer touchpoints, meetings, and engagement levels
- **Issue Resolution**: Problem identification and resolution effectiveness
- **Goal Achievement**: Progress against individual and team objectives

#### Process Performance Metrics
- **Onboarding Efficiency**: Time to value and activation rate improvements
- **Support Quality**: Resolution time, satisfaction, and escalation rates
- **Resource Utilization**: Team capacity, workload distribution, and productivity
- **Training Effectiveness**: Educational program impact and engagement
- **Tool Adoption**: Technology utilization and efficiency gains

### Predictive Analytics and Insights

#### Churn Prediction Modeling
- **Risk Scoring**: Probability of churn based on behavioral patterns
- **Early Warning Systems**: Automated alerts for at-risk accounts
- **Intervention Effectiveness**: Success rate of retention activities
- **Segment Analysis**: Churn patterns by customer characteristics
- **Timeline Modeling**: Predicted timing of potential churn events

#### Growth Opportunity Identification
- **Expansion Potential**: Upselling and cross-selling opportunity scoring
- **Feature Gap Analysis**: Unused capabilities with high value potential
- **Integration Opportunities**: Workflow automation and efficiency gains
- **Market Expansion**: New use case and department expansion potential
- **Referral Potential**: Customer advocacy and reference program eligibility
