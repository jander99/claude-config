# Market Analysis Framework

## Market Sizing and Opportunity Assessment

### Total Addressable Market (TAM) Analysis
- **Top-down Approach**: Industry reports, government statistics, third-party market research
- **Bottom-up Approach**: Customer segments, pricing models, and adoption rate projections
- **Value-based Sizing**: Customer willingness to pay and economic value proposition analysis
- **Growth Rate Projections**: Historical trends, industry forecasts, and market driver analysis

### Market Segmentation Strategy
- **Demographic Segmentation**: Age, income, geographic, and firmographic characteristics
- **Behavioral Segmentation**: Usage patterns, purchase behavior, and customer lifecycle stage
- **Psychographic Segmentation**: Values, attitudes, lifestyle, and decision-making preferences
- **Needs-based Segmentation**: Pain points, job-to-be-done, and value proposition alignment

### Competitive Landscape Mapping

#### Direct Competitor Analysis
- **Feature Comparison Matrix**: Side-by-side product capability assessment
- **Pricing Strategy Evaluation**: Price points, packaging, and value proposition analysis
- **Market Position Assessment**: Brand strength, market share, and competitive advantages
- **Customer Satisfaction Analysis**: Review analysis, NPS scores, and churn rate comparison

#### Indirect Competitor and Substitute Analysis
- **Alternative Solution Mapping**: Non-traditional competitors and workaround solutions
- **Switching Cost Analysis**: Barriers to adoption and migration complexity
- **Disruption Risk Assessment**: Emerging technologies and business model innovations
- **Blue Ocean Opportunities**: Uncontested market spaces and value innovation potential

## Industry Analysis Framework

### Porter's Five Forces Analysis
1. **Threat of New Entrants**: Barriers to entry, capital requirements, regulatory constraints
2. **Bargaining Power of Suppliers**: Supplier concentration, switching costs, integration risks
3. **Bargaining Power of Buyers**: Customer concentration, price sensitivity, switching costs
4. **Threat of Substitute Products**: Alternative solutions, performance-price trade-offs
5. **Rivalry Among Competitors**: Market concentration, differentiation, exit barriers

### Market Trend Analysis
- **Technology Trends**: Emerging technologies, adoption curves, and innovation cycles
- **Regulatory Trends**: Policy changes, compliance requirements, and industry standards
- **Economic Trends**: Market conditions, investment climate, and economic indicators
- **Social Trends**: Demographic shifts, behavioral changes, and cultural factors

## Customer Analysis Methods

### Customer Journey Mapping
- **Awareness Stage**: How customers discover solutions and evaluate options
- **Consideration Stage**: Decision criteria, evaluation process, and influencer roles
- **Purchase Stage**: Buying process, decision makers, and conversion factors
- **Retention Stage**: Onboarding, usage patterns, and renewal/expansion opportunities
- **Advocacy Stage**: Referral behavior, testimonials, and community engagement

### Voice of Customer (VoC) Analysis
- **Customer Interview Framework**: Structured questioning techniques for insight gathering
- **Survey Design**: Question formulation, scale selection, and response bias mitigation
- **Feedback Analysis**: Text analysis, sentiment scoring, and theme identification
- **Customer Segmentation**: Persona development and needs-based clustering

## Market Entry and Go-to-Market Strategy

### Market Entry Strategy Assessment
- **Entry Mode Selection**: Direct entry, partnerships, acquisitions, or licensing
- **Timing Analysis**: Market readiness, competitive window, and resource availability
- **Resource Requirements**: Investment needs, capability gaps, and timeline assessment
- **Risk Assessment**: Market risks, competitive response, and mitigation strategies

### Go-to-Market Channel Analysis
- **Channel Partner Evaluation**: Partner capabilities, market reach, and alignment assessment
- **Direct vs. Indirect Sales**: Cost analysis, control considerations, and scalability factors
- **Digital Marketing Strategy**: Online channels, content strategy, and conversion optimization
- **Sales Process Design**: Lead generation, qualification, and conversion methodologies
